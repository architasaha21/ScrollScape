ScrollScape


ScrollScape is a smooth parallax scrolling experience crafted with pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It's designed to demonstrate how motion and depth can transform simple web pages into immersive digital spaces.


This project is lightweight, responsive, and minimal — perfect for learning the fundamentals of modern front-end design without any external libraries. Every scroll reveals a new visual layer, creating a dynamic and engaging narrative flow.


Whether you're exploring web animation or prototyping creative ideas, ScrollScape offers a solid starting point. Dive into the code, explore the effects, and see how far simple technologies can take you.
